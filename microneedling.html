Collagen Reboot Therapy

Microneedling & DermaPen™ employs ultra-fine, medical-grade needles to create controlled micro-channels in the skin. This triggers your body’s healing cascade, boosting collagen and elastin production for visibly firmer, more resilient skin.

Ideal for smoothing fine lines, reducing acne scars, and refining enlarged pores, this minimally invasive treatment delivers cumulative improvement with each session. Customized needle depths ensure comfort and safety, while topical serums penetrate more deeply for enhanced hydration and repair.
